Website Evaluations

Ling's Car

Lings Cars is a website for selling cars. The website has been created for the purpose of selling cars.

  • Contrast: The website utilizes a mix of different colors and a vibrant background, creating poor contrast.
  • Repetition: The website lacks repetition in any form
  • Alignment: The website appears a bit disorganized, with content scattered throughout.
  • Proximity: All the cars available for lease are grouped together, but information about the company is scattered.

YouTube

YouTube is a website where people can upload their content and share it with the world.

  • Contrast: YouTube uses a white background for its interface, which provides a high level of contrast against the colorful thumbnails of videos, making them stand out.
  • Repetition: The navigation bar at the top of the page is repeated across all pages, providing consistent access to essential features and maintaining user familiarity.
  • Alignment: The content on YouTube is aligned in a grid format, ensuring consistent spacing and alignment between thumbnails, titles, and descriptions, contributing to a clean and organized appearance.
  • Proximity: When you are watching a video, the recommendations are grouped on the right, and the comments are grouped at the bottom.
